Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy explains what information PGL Marketing collects when you use our website or contact us, how we use it, and the choices you have.

PGL Marketing is operated by Personal Growth Lab LLC (doing business as PGL Marketing), based in San Diego, California. In this policy, "we", "us", and "our" refer to PGL Marketing. By using our website or submitting a form, you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

We collect two kinds of information:

  • Information you give us. When you fill out a form on our site, you may share your name, email address, phone number, business name or website, your niche, your monthly ad spend range, and any message you write. When you book a call, we also collect the details you provide to schedule it.
  • Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we use cookies and similar technologies, including the Meta (Facebook) Pixel and analytics tools, to understand how visitors use the site. This may include your device type, browser, pages viewed, referring source, and general location derived from your IP address.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiry and schedule your strategy call.
  • To provide, run, and improve our marketing services for you.
  • To send you information you asked for and relevant follow-ups about your inquiry.
  • To measure and improve our website and our advertising.
  • To meet legal, tax, and security obligations.

How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with:

  • Service providers that help us operate, such as our CRM and scheduling platform (GoHighLevel), our form and email delivery providers, and hosting and analytics providers. They may only use your data to provide services to us.
  • Advertising platforms such as Meta, through their pixel, to measure and improve our ads. We do not share the content of your messages with them.
  • Authorities if required by law, or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

Cookies and tracking

We use cookies and pixels to run the site, remember your choices, and measure our marketing. You can control cookies through your browser settings. To opt out of interest-based advertising, you can adjust your ad settings on the relevant platform, for example in your Meta account.

Your choices and rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, and you can opt out of marketing follow-ups at any time by replying to any message or emailing us. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights.

California residents. Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request that we delete it, and to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell your personal information. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

Data retention and security

We keep your information only as long as we need it for the purposes above or as required by law, then delete or anonymize it. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, though no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Other websites

Our site may link to other websites we do not control. This policy does not apply to those sites, and we are not responsible for their practices.

Children

Our site and services are meant for businesses and are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the date at the top of this page.
