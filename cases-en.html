05 / 05· Tattoo

Selling across
a language barrier

$2.9CPL
Cost per qualified lead
6days
From launch to first booked leads
Client
Tattoo studio in San Diego serving the English-speaking market
Starting point
Owner doesn't speak English. Conventional path (English-speaking presenter casting + shoot) meant a week of casting, $500+ per day and the risk the audience wouldn't accept a stand-in.
Our work
One studio shoot day with the owner in his native language. An AI avatar voiced the clips in clean American English. Meta Ads driving messaging conversations, with a ManyChat auto-funnel qualifying every lead on four filters (tattoo size, budget, timing, references) before a manager replies.
Result
$2.9 cost per qualified lead. First booked leads six days after launch. 148 messaging conversations from one campaign ($428.53 spent), with AI-voiced clips so natural the comments never asked "is that a real person?".
