FAQ

Questions before the call

How fast can we launch?
Fast. A first campaign can be live in as little as 6 days, and the full system usually comes together within the first 30. We nail down the exact timeline on the call.
Do I have to be on camera?
Most founders want to be on camera, and it's our favorite way to work, one shoot day a month on location and we make it easy. Not your thing? We have AI-driven options too.
What if the numbers don't move?
We guarantee the system, not luck. Within 30 days you'll have a live system bringing in real inquiries or we refund our fee, and you keep the audit and 30-day plan either way.
What's the minimum ad budget?
Ad spend goes straight to Meta or Google, separate from our retainer. We size the right starting budget together on the call, based on your target cost per customer.
Who does the work?
A dedicated core team: a producer on your account, a scripts & edit lead, a paid-ads specialist, and the founders on strategy. No interns, no offshore factories. You meet them on day one.
