01

The client

Wellness Embassy is a holistic lymphatic and facial studio in Sorrento Valley, San Diego, founded by Angela, a licensed esthetician with a signature author's method across lymphatic, buccal, myofascial, and sculpting work. The offering is genuinely differentiated.

What it did not have was a system to fill the calendar predictably, keep clients coming back, and prove where every dollar went. Our mandate was to own the whole growth stack, from the first ad a stranger sees to the tenth visit of a loyal client, and make all of it measurable.

02

What we built

Six connected systems. Each one works on its own; together they form a loop where paid traffic becomes a booked client, a booked client becomes a repeat client, and every step reports back with real numbers.

01 · Acquisition
Paid traffic and content
  • Meta and Instagram ad campaigns, geo-targeted to San Diego, Rancho Santa Fe, La Jolla and Del Mar, with retargeting.
  • Content engine: Reels and ad scripts written in Angela's expert voice, education first, not brochure copy.
  • Organic growth compounding the paid work: 91 posts and a library of high-performing educational Reels.
02 · Analytics
End-to-end attribution
  • Full-funnel tracking: ad, click, booking and revenue, tied together.
  • UTM taxonomy plus first and last-touch attribution, so we know which ad brought which lead.
  • Weekly reports: spend, bookings by source, bookings by day, and return on ad spend, with concrete changes proposed from the numbers.
03 · Operations
CRM and booking backbone
  • GoHighLevel set up as the single source of truth: calendars, services and booking pages.
  • Booking flow hardened: reschedule confirmations, corrected pricing and staff data-access controls.
  • Custom fields for visit count, feedback score and client history.
04 · Messaging
Follow-ups and compliance
  • Automated SMS confirmations and reminders for every appointment.
  • A2P 10DLC brand and campaign registration plus a WhatsApp channel for deliverability.
  • Instagram welcome DM for new followers, plus hosted privacy, terms and refund pages with proper consent.
05 · Retention
Loyalty engine
  • 10-visit loyalty program with automatic visit counting and gifts at visits 1, 4, 7 and 10.
  • Pre-bonus nudge: an automated message when a client is one visit from a reward.
  • A live digital loyalty card: one permanent link per client that always shows current progress, plus printed in-studio cards. Rewards without discounting, so the ticket and tips stay whole.
06 · Experience
Feedback and consultation
  • Post-visit feedback flow with a 1 to 10 score, routing happy clients toward public reviews.
  • A digital consultation record on every client: goals, health flags, contraindications, techniques and plan.
  • Client history that follows the person, so every visit is personalized.

04

The approach

We did not run ads and hand over a report. We built the entire path a client travels, made every step measurable, and grew the studio on value, never on discounts.

Principle
Grown on value, not discounts

No deep discounts, no bait pricing, no cutting the experience. We sold the studio as a premium, one-of-a-kind solution. The results hold because clients were never trained to wait for a sale.

Principle
Loyalty that protects the ticket

We deliberately rejected bulk prepaid packages, the easy move most studios make. Bulk drags the average ticket down and removes tips. Our program rewards clients along the way instead, so they feel valued while the ticket stays whole.

Principle
Optimized from the numbers

Because the full funnel is tracked, we do not guess. Every week we read spend, sources and returns, then propose specific changes. The strategy adjusts to what the data actually shows.
